C-E Electric: Expert Electrical Contracting Services in Texas started with a single truck in 1998, when founder Carl Edwards decided Fort Worth needed an electrician who showed up on time and explained the work in plain language before touching a wire. Twenty five years later there are six trucks and a small office off Industrial Blvd, but the approach hasn't changed.

We pull permits ourselves, walk every inspection with the city, and train our own apprentices instead of hiring day labor for a job. If a panel is undersized or a circuit is overloaded, we'll tell you straight, with a written quote before anyone picks up a drill.

Texas heat is hard on wiring and harder on outdated panels. Most of our calls start with a homeowner who noticed a warm outlet or a breaker that trips for no obvious reason. We diagnose before we quote, so you're not paying to replace parts that were never the problem.

What we do

Electrical work, done to code

Residential Wiring & Panel Upgrades

From knob-and-tube replacement in older Fort Worth bungalows to 200-amp service upgrades for a growing family, we handle wiring projects big and small. Every job passes city inspection on the first try.

Commercial Electrical Contracting

Restaurants, retail buildouts, warehouses, office tenant improvements. We work around your operating hours and keep the paperwork straight for your landlord, your insurer, and the city inspector.

Backup Generator Installation

Texas grid trouble isn't going away. We size, install, and service standby generators so a summer storm doesn't mean a week without air conditioning or a fridge full of spoiled food.

Lighting & EV Charger Installation

Recessed lighting retrofits, landscape lighting, and Level 2 EV charger installs for the driveway. We walk the property with you before we write a single number on a quote.
